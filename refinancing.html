Refinancing

Perhaps you need extra money for investments or renovations, are selling your property, would like more flexibility or are unhappy with your current lender. Refinancing may be just the option you need but it is important to note that it isn't always the answer.

When considering whether or not to refinance it is important to ascertain the clear long term benefit whilst taking into account the costs involved, in the form of exit and break fees, among others.

If you are considering refinancing make sure you do your research, or better still let one of our consultants do it for you. With access to a range of home loans we can provide you with a detailed overview of your refinancing options. If you need more information on these types of home loan products and how they suit your individual home loan needs, our mortgage consultants can help.
