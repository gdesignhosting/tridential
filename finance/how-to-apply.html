What We Need From You

When applying for finance with Tridential, you need to provide the following standard information to us (we may ask for more information depending on your individual circumstances):

  • details about your existing home loan (if any)
  • the amount you want to borrow
  • details about your assets and liabilities
  • details about your ongoing expenses and regular payments
  • details of property
